1 Email Alerts

1.1 Follow a paper or an author

  1. Visit Microsoft Academic https://academic.microsoft.com/php.
  2. Search a paper or an author.
  3. To Follow a paper or an author, then you will receive Live Mail email alert for changes.
  4. You can do it in a similar way on Goolge Scholar.

1.2 Follow a keyword or a paper

  1. Visit Web of Science http://apps.webofknowledge.com/.
  2. Search a paper tile, an author or a keyword.
  3. You can creat an alert for the search.
  4. Or Click an item for a paper, Create Citation Alert for the paper.

1.3 Follow a journal

  1. Visit the Homepage of a journal, such as JPCC.
  2. To find words like e-Alerts or Email Alert to creat email alerts (login maybe needed).

2 RSS

  • You can filter news in RSS by keywords, while you can not do that on e-alerts.

2.1 RSS Source

  • Get RSS source from a journal website or a search engine like Google or Bing.

  • P.S. RSS sources form http://feeds.feedburner.com/ are often Unavailable in China, you can mix these source on RSS Mix into a new source.

2.2 RSS Reader

2.2.1 Chrome Extension (Recommend)

  • FeedBro

2.2.2 Standalone App

  • Quite RSS

2.2.3 Online

  • Feedly+Feedly filtering and sorting (a Chrome extension) (Feedly Often Unavailable in China)
  • InoReader (Also often Unavailable in China)
  • Self Hosted Tiny Tiny RSS
