1.1 Setup

1.1.1 Main

  • Task: NSW, IBRION
  • Quality: PREC
  • Functional: GGA
  • Spin Polarized: ISPIN, MAGMOM
  • LDA+U: LDAU (暂定)
  • Charge: NELECT=N(中性）+charge, N(中性）有POTCAR得出

1.1.2 More

1.1.2.1 OPt

  • Optimize Cell: ISIF
  • Energy:EDIFFG(>0)
  • Max Force: EDIFFG(<0)
  • Max Displament: POTIM
  • Option->Algorithm: IBRION

1.1.3 MD

  • Ensemble (NVE/NVT): TEBEG,TEEND,SMASS
  • Timestep: POTIM
  • Simulation Time: NSW*POTIM

1.2 Electronic

1.2.1 Main

  • Energy Cutoff: ENCUT (受PREC控制，默认值从POTCAR读取)
  • SCF Tolerance: EDIFF
  • K-point set: KPOINTS or KSPACING=0.1; KGAMMA=.TRUE.
  • Pseudopotentials: X X_sv X_pv (PAW)
  • Pseudopotential representation: LREAL
  • vdw correction: IVDW

1.2.2 More

  • Max SCF Cycles: NELM
  • Min SCF Cycles: NELMIN
  • Orbital occupancy: ISMEAR, SIGMA
  • Dipole correction: LDIPOL,IDIPOL
  • KPOINTS Setting: Seraration->KSPACING, or custom grid parameter->KPOINTS
